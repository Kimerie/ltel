The Story So Far...

An evil toy brought to life by the powers of dark magic is on the loose, spreading its evil throughout the Crystal Isles. Now is the time for you to travel to the islands of Crystal Casters to save the corrupted wizards from this evil spell!

Adventure awaits you in Crystal Casters, the FREE card battle and puzzle RPG game that will blow you away!

With over 600 different cards and tons of quests to play through, Crystal Casters provides enough content for hours and hours of unique, brain-twisting gameplay.

Exciting Features

QUESTS
Your fantasy adventure starts now with over 150 quests. Face-off with challenging enemies and defeat them to collect their precious loot!
UNIQUE PUZZLE-BASED GAMEPLAY
Connect crystals to cast powerful magic spells. The bigger the chain, the bigger the spell! Develop the ultimate strategy to create insane matches and combos to defeat your enemies!
CREATE A TEAM OF FIGHTERS
Over 500 collectible cards with unique stats and skills that you can use in battle! Create a team of deadly fighters to take on the dark forces of magic.
